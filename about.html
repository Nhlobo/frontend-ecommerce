About Premium Hair Wigs & Extensions

Empowering beauty and confidence through premium quality hair products since our inception

Our Story

Premium Hair Wigs & Extensions Pty (Ltd) was born from a passion for empowering individuals through beautiful, high-quality hair products. Based in the vibrant heart of Protea Glen, Soweto, Johannesburg, we've grown to become one of Gauteng's most trusted providers of premium hair products.

What started as a vision to bring authentic, luxurious hair products to South Africa has evolved into a thriving business committed to quality, customer satisfaction, and authenticity. We understand that your hair is your crown, and we're dedicated to helping you wear it with confidence.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, premium-quality hair products that enhance natural beauty and boost confidence. We strive to offer an exceptional shopping experience backed by expert advice, reliable service, and authentic products that stand the test of time.
