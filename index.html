About Us

Welcome to Premium Hair Wigs & Extensions

Premium Hair Wigs & Extensions Pty (Ltd) is your premier destination for high-quality hair wigs, extensions, and accessories. Based in the heart of Protea Glen, Soweto, Johannesburg, we pride ourselves on offering authentic, luxurious hair products that help you look and feel your best.

Our Story

Founded with a passion for empowering individuals through beautiful hair, we have grown to become one of Gauteng's most trusted providers of premium hair products. Our commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and authenticity sets us apart in the industry.

Our Commitment

We are CIPC and VAT registered, ensuring complete transparency and compliance with South African business regulations. We are also POPIA compliant, safeguarding your personal information at every step.

Quality Assurance

All our products are made from 100% authentic human hair, sourced responsibly and crafted with care. We offer a 7-day return policy and provide fast delivery within 2-5 business days across South Africa.

Customer First

Your satisfaction is our priority. We offer free shipping on orders above R1,500, secure PayFast payment integration, and dedicated customer support to assist you at every stage of your shopping journey.
